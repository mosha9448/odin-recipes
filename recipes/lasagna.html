Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian baked dish made with layers of pasta sheets, rich meat sauce, and lots of cheese.

It’s filling, tasty, and perfect for lunch or dinner with family.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Minced meat (chicken or beef)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onion and garlic.
  2. Add minced meat and cook until it turns brown.
  3. Add tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  4. Boil lasagna sheets (if required by the package instructions).
  5. In a baking dish, add a layer of sauce, then pasta sheets, then cheese.
  6. Repeat the layers until everything is used.
  7. Bake at 180°C for 30–40 minutes.
  8. Let it cool for 10 minutes, then slice and serve.
