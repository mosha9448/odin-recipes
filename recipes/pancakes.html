Pancakes

Description

Pancakes are soft and fluffy breakfast cakes made using flour, milk, and eggs.

They are quick to prepare and taste great with honey, syrup, or fruits.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 pinch salt
  • Butter (for cooking)

Steps

  1. Mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add milk and egg, then mix into a smooth batter.
  3. Heat a pan and add a little butter.
  4. Pour batter onto the pan and cook until bubbles appear.
  5. Flip the pancake and cook the other side.
  6. Repeat for remaining batter.
  7. Serve warm with syrup or fruits.
