Chicken Biryani

Description

Chicken Biryani is a famous Indian rice dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and aromatic spices.

It is flavorful, spicy, and perfect for lunch or special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Chicken
  • Onion
  • Tomato
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Biryani masala
  • Mint leaves
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt
  • Oil

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 20 minutes.
  2. Fry onions in oil until golden brown.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and saut√© for 1 minute.
  4. Add chicken and cook until it changes color.
  5. Add tomatoes, yogurt, biryani masala, and salt. Cook well.
  6. Boil rice until 70% cooked and drain the water.
  7. Layer chicken and rice in a pot.
  8. Cover and steam (dum) for 15 minutes.
  9. Serve hot with raita.
